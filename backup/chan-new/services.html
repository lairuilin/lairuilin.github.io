Academic Services

  2. International Scientific Committee member of Kaleidoscope, Network of Excellence in Technology Enhanced Learning of European Union Commission.

  5. Executive Committee Member of International Artificial Intelligence in Education Society.
  6. Coordinator (1994-2002), Information Technology in Education Program of National Science Council.
